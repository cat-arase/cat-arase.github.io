Description

I have done a few basic projects involving blacksmithing after taking a brief class my freshman year. I have made a few hooks, a leaf keychain, a bottle opener, and some chain. I unfortunately do not have pictures of all of them.

Lessons Learned

Blacksmithing is an art and a very slow process. Also, it’s a workout. I feel I developed a greater appreciation of metalworking tools.
