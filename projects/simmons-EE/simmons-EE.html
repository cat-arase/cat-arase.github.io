Simmons EE Lab Chair (2021)

Simmons EE Lab part way through clean up

Description

I was in charge of the EE lab in my dorm my senior fall. I was restored multiple 3D printers, got our laser cutter approved with Environmental and health services, cleaned out projects from before the pandemic and acquired new tables and chairs. I also ran weekly orientations to get more people access to the shop. Unfortunately, I forgot to take before and after pictures.
