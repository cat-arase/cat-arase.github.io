Electrocatalytic Cell (2020)

Electrocatalytic cell in use

Description

This cell was designed for my research project with the MIT Electrochemical Energy lab.This cell is used to do high throughput testing on electrocatalysts. Samples are placed on a conductive glass slide. This slide needed to protrude from the cell for a wire to be connected to it. A gasket seals this off from the rest of the cell. Two probes needed to be submerged in the testing solution; these can be seen in the top of the cell. The cell is made from Teflon to withstand various testing conditions.

Lessons Learned

I learned about designing something from external specifications. I learned about ordering parts from a machine shop.
