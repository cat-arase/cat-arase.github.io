Clock (2019)

Key Achievements

  • Laser engraved clock
  • Image editing
  • Sanding

Description

This clock was a birthday present for my friend from Washington. This clock was laser cut. I used photoshop to take the cutout of Washington and a picture of Mt. Rainier to create a layout for the clock. I bought a clock set online for the hands.

Lessons Learned

In making this project, I was very focused on making it aesthetically pleasing. I did not think about the center of mass of the clock and how that would affect how it sat on the wall. I had to glue on washers to the back of the clock to keep it from tipping.
