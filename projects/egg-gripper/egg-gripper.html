Compliant Egg Gripper (2022)

The final egg gripper made from delrin

Key Achievements

  • Successfully gripped eggs without breaking them
  • Designed using flexure mechanisms for smooth motion
  • Iterated through multiple design prototypes

Motivation

A group homework project for my flexure design class. The objective was to design a compliant mechanism capable of safely gripping an egg.
